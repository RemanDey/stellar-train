About

StellarTrain is a fast, secure, and lightweight payment transfer platform built on the Stellar blockchain. It enables seamless XLM (Lumen) fund transfers between Stellar accounts — combining the power of decentralized finance with a clean, beginner-friendly interface.

Key Features

  • Instant XLM Transfers: Send and receive funds within seconds using the Stellar network.
  • Low Transaction Fees: Enjoy lightning-fast payments with minimal blockchain costs.
  • Secure & Transparent: Every transaction is cryptographically signed and recorded on the public Stellar ledger.
  • Simple & Efficient: Designed for smooth user experience — no complex wallet setups or confusing steps.
  • Cross-Border Payments: Send money globally without traditional banking delays or fees.
  • Built on Stellar: Leverages Stellar's robust infrastructure for reliability.

Powered by Stellar

StellarTrain uses the Stellar Horizon API and SDK to handle key management, transaction creation, and broadcast.
It automatically manages account sequences, network fees, and real-time balance checks to ensure reliability and accuracy in every transfer.

Our Goal

StellarTrain was created to simplify blockchain payments and help developers, students, and enthusiasts understand how decentralized finance systems work in practice. Whether you’re sending funds for a project, a reward system, or experimenting with blockchain apps, StellarTrain keeps it simple, transparent, and educational.
